Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

SitCheck helps people find and contribute restroom information, including location, bidet availability, cleanliness, and access fee details. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

SitCheck may collect or process the following information:

  • Location data: If you allow location access, the app uses your device location to center the map, show your position on the map, and help you find nearby restroom listings. While the app is open and location is enabled, SitCheck may receive updated location readings from your device to keep your map position current and support features such as nearest listings. These updates are used on your device for display and are not sent to our servers unless you submit a restroom location using the contribution form.
  • Submitted restroom information: When you contribute a listing, the app may store the restroom name, location description, map coordinates, bidet availability, cleanliness rating, water pressure, payment requirement, fee, and notes you provide.
  • Local app data: The app may use browser storage on your device for basic app behavior, such as remembering temporary map or session state and optional notification preferences.
  • Admin account data: Moderator/admin access, where available, uses Supabase authentication to manage submitted listings.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the app's core features, including displaying restroom listings on a map, filtering listings, showing and updating your approximate position on the map while you use the app, centering the map on your location, reviewing community submissions, and improving the usefulness of the restroom directory.

Location Permission

Location access is optional. If you deny location permission, you can still browse the map and use the app manually. SitCheck does not track your location when the app is closed or running in the background. While you are actively using the app with location enabled, your position may update on the map as you move.

Contribution Reminders

Contribution reminders are on by default. SitCheck may schedule local notifications on your device to remind you to add restroom listings after you have not opened the app for about 12 hours. These reminders are scheduled and delivered on your device only. SitCheck does not use a push notification server for this feature, and you can turn reminders off anytime in About, which cancels pending reminders. Contribution reminders are separate from location access and are not used for continuous location tracking.

Third-Party Services

SitCheck uses third-party services that may process information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: Used to store and retrieve approved restroom listings and submitted contributions.
  • OpenStreetMap and Leaflet map tiles: Used to display the map and location context.
  • Google Fonts: Used to load app typography.
  • Ko-fi: An optional external support link. Opening it takes you outside SitCheck.

Sharing of Information

Approved restroom listings and related details may be shown publicly in the app. We do not sell personal information. Submitted listings may be reviewed by app moderators before becoming public.

Data Retention and Deletion

Submitted restroom information may be retained for as long as it is useful for the SitCheck directory. If you want to request removal of a submitted listing or ask privacy-related questions, contact the developer using the contact information provided on the SitCheck Google Play Store listing.

Children's Privacy

SitCheck is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact the developer so the information can be reviewed or removed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The latest version will be made available through the app listing or the app's public website.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer using the contact email listed on the SitCheck Google Play Store listing.
